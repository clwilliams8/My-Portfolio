Summary

This multi-phase engagement for a prestigious University of Arkansas institution encompassed three core deliverables:

  1. Platform Modernization: Transitioned from WordPress to a custom Laravel-based CMS designed for intuitive content management, significantly reducing training overhead for administrative staff.
  2. Content Migration: Executed bulk migration of 2,000+ blog posts from the legacy platform while preserving content integrity and URL structures.
  3. Payment Integration: Developed a secure donation portal integrated with CyberSource (Visa) payment processing, supporting both one-time and recurring contributions to designated scholarship funds.

I architected and implemented the backend CMS modules enabling full CRUD operations across all content types. Beyond initial development, I managed ongoing ADA accessibility compliance using Monsido scanning tools, implementing monthly remediation cycles to maintain WCAG standards.

This project demonstrates my ability to deliver enterprise-grade solutions for higher education institutions with stringent accessibility and security requirements.
