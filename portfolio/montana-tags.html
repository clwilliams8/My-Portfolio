About MontanaTags

Across the entire US, MontanaTags.com eliminates sales tax and personal property tax on your vehicle or Vessel with our online registration service. Experience Montana's freedoms, bringing 100% tax savings to you!

About The Project

Mission: Build a greenfield e-commerce platform from the ground up for this Montana vehicle registration service preparing for a major marketing investment. The client required a modern Shopify implementation with a custom tax savings calculator to help prospective customers understand the financial benefits of Montana registration for their vehicles and vessels.

Technologies Used: Shopify (E-Commerce Platform), Shopify Liquid (Templating Language), Custom JavaScript (Calculator Logic), Responsive Design, SEO Optimization.
