Hank's Fine Furniture App

About Hank's

Founded in 1975 by Hank Browne, Hank's Fine Furniture has grown to 14 stores in 4 states and is one of America's Top 100 Independent Furniture Retailers. From the very beginning, Hank's Fine Furniture's success has been built on offering quality furniture, bedding and accessories at excellent values. As a family-owned business, Hank's Fine Furniture has thrived because of our dedication to service after the sale. You, the customer, have been the most important part of our business for over 40 years.

About The Project

Mission: Develop and deploy a cross-platform mobile application to enhance customer engagement through loyalty programs, promotional content, and streamlined access to delivery tracking and financing options.

Technologies Used: GoodBarber (Cross-Platform App Builder), Progressive Web App (PWA) Architecture, HTML5 & CSS3 (Custom Page Development), Adobe Photoshop (Asset Creation), REST API Integration (Delivery Tracking).

Summary

This project required balancing rapid deployment timelines with custom functionality requirements. After conducting a build-vs-buy analysis, I selected GoodBarber's Progressive Web App platform for its cross-platform capabilities and streamlined App Store submission process—a critical consideration given Apple's rigorous review requirements for app quality and user value.

While the platform provided robust pre-built components, I developed custom HTML/CSS modules to meet specific business requirements. This included a Delivery Tracking page integrated with the company's logistics API, enabling customers to monitor their furniture orders in real-time by entering their order number. I also built a Financing portal providing streamlined access to payment options. Additionally, I created all launch screen assets using Adobe Photoshop, ensuring brand consistency across iOS, Android, phone, and tablet form factors.

The application was successfully published to both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store within two weeks of project initiation—demonstrating effective technology selection, efficient execution, and the ability to extend platform capabilities through custom development when business requirements demand it.
